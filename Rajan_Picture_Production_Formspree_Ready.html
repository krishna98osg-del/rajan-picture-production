About

BEHIND
THE LENS

I'm Rajan — the founder and lead photographer at Rajan Picture Production. What started as shooting friends' weddings on weekends has grown into a full-time practice across weddings, portraits and travel work, all built on the same belief: a photograph should feel like the moment, not just look like it.

No two shoots get the same treatment. A monsoon wedding in Kerala asks for different instincts than a studio portrait in Delhi — I show up having actually thought about which one I'm walking into.

How I work

The approach

listen

A real conversation first

Before any camera comes out, I want to know who you are and what this day or session actually means to you — that's what shapes the shot list.

observe

Candid over choreographed

I'll direct when a shot needs it, but most of my favourite frames happen when people forget I'm in the room.

deliver

A fast, honest edit

You get a curated gallery, not a dump of a thousand near-duplicates — edited for the story, not just exposure.
